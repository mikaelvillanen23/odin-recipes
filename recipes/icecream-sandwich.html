Ice Cream Sandwich

Description

Why not spoil yourself this Sunday with a decadent Sundae?

Ingredients

For this recipe you will need the following:

  • Two fresh buns of your choice
  • Some ice cream
  • Optional: Caramel sauce, and perhaps a strawberry or two

Steps

This recipe is pretty straightforward:

  1. Cut buns in half and place on a plate
  2. Scoop a bit too much ice cream in the middle of each
  3. If desired, pour some caramel sauce on top and place a neatly cut strawberry half next to each bun
  4. Enjoy to your heart's content!
